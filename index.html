HOLC Redlining Layer

Historical HOLC redlining boundaries are available from the Mapping Inequality project (University of Richmond). Download the Chicago GeoJSON and add it to data/holc_chicago.geojson to enable this layer.

Chicago's redlining history is central to this analysis: majority-Black neighborhoods on the South and West Sides received "D" (Hazardous) grades, contributing to disinvestment and the destruction of Black-owned cemeteries.
